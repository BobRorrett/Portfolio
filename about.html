About Me

I was born and raised in Suffolk, I graduated with a first-class integrated master's degree in 2022, from the University of Bath, studying Integrated Design Engineering. I moved to Glasgow for work, and I am loving everything that Scotland has to offer, doing lots of hiking, camping, fishing and surfing.
I have spent the last year and a half working for a chemistry automation startup, developing custom hardware, mostly electronics, for their automation platforms.
In my spare time, I enjoy painting and drawing. I strive to translate this creativity into my engineering work. I am a strong believer that the more diverse set of experiences you have in your life, the more links you can draw between seemingly unrelated principles, which will enable more creative and elegant solutions to problems and challenges that you encounter.

Thank you for taking the time to look through my portfolio. I hope you have found something of interest here.
