University engineering projects

C3PU, an autonomous portable platform for universal chemical synthesis

Brief:

  • Make case for to make C3PU, to be presented in front of an audience including Jeff Bezos at his annual MARS conference
  • It must be visually pleasing
  • Designed and built from scratch in 2 weeks; It was getting shipped to America at the end of these 2 weeks, so it had to be finished
  • Had to be easily to assemble and transport
  • Access required to top, sides and back

Concept development

The Desgin

Laser cut Acrylic panels attach to C3PU's frame and anchor the rest of the case.
3 rolled Aluminium sheet parts for front plate, and the removable lid and back plate.

Lid and back plate are removable, held in position by magnets. The front aluminium plate is secured by the shape of the front panel, meaning there are no visible fasteners.
3d printed trim and front panel for aesthetics.

Outcome

C3PU at the MARS conference, featuring the case
